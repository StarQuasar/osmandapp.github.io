2021 New Year resolutions

Dec 31, 2020

Happy New 2021 Year!

How in 2019 and 2020 New Year resolutions we describe our achievements and prepare a new list for 2021.

2020 achievements

2020 is the Covid-19 pandemic year and it is a very difficult year for the travel industry. All this year we worked for our users and made many useful . Year to year we see the growth of active users, this year is no exception.

In June 2020 our company celebrated 10 years old. We made a Mega Sale with 90% off and sent out more than 150 T-shirts to our users.

Android

OsmAnd Android got 4 major releases this year: 3.6, 3.7, 3.8 and 3.9 (already available for Beta Test) with more than 300 public features and issues closed on Github. Here are the most prominent features of 2020.

  • Plugins & UI customization (2020 NYR)
    • Wikipedia
    • Drawer customization
    • POI Categories
  • Profiles (2020 NYR)
  • New slopes / hillshade maps (2020 NYR)
  • Export/import all settings and data
  • User-selected icons for Favorites
  • Analyze on Map
  • "Plan a route" tool
  • Navigation: Direct-to-point, Exit number
  • Bicycle nodes
  • Analyze on Map
  • Track approximation

    iOS

    We’ve managed to release 4 new versions of OsmAnd for iOS this year: 3.12, 3.14, 3.80, 3.90 (already available for Beta Test)! All this was possible due to the commitment and hard work of our iOS team to catch up with Android..

  • Public Transport and Route details (2020 NYR)
  • New slopes / hillshade maps (2020 NYR)
  • Profiles (2020 NYR)
  • Plan a route (2020 NYR)

    New Year Resolutions 2021

    We are going to start next year with an extended team of 7 software engineers. We grew 20% compared to the previous year and it is good news. We can probably do 10% more in 2020 :-)

    Our list is growing every year and, as of today, we have around 1500 requests waiting to be fixed or implemented. That’s why, it is important for us to set up some public goals and try to follow them to a certain extent.

    GENERAL

  • Travel and Track Collections (Android & iOS)
  • Full export / import and online Backup (Android & iOS)
  • Improve Tracks UI & Navigation with Altitude Graph (Android & iOS)
  • Plan Route with Track Approximation (follow up on iOS)
  • Custom user icons for Favorites (follow up on iOS)
  • Plugins & UI Customization (follow up on iOS)
  • Quick Action Panel (Android)

    Sure, hundreds of small features didn’t get to this list though they’re going to be implemented in 2021.
